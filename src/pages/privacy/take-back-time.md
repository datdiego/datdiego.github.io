# Take Back Time — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 5, 2026

## Overview

Take Back Time is a phone usage tracking app that runs entirely on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage data is sent to any server
- No analytics or tracking tools are used
- No advertisements are displayed
- No third-party SDKs that collect data are included
- No account creation is required

## Permissions

Take Back Time requests a single permission:

- **Usage Access** (`PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS`): Read-only access to app usage statistics via Android's UsageStatsManager API. This data is queried locally on your device and never leaves it.

## Data Storage

All data stays on your device. The app queries Android's built-in usage statistics and displays them to you. No data is written to external storage, cloud services, or remote databases.

## Third-Party Services

Take Back Time does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

Take Back Time does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
